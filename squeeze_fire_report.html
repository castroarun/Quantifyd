How it works: The system uses Bollinger Band width and trend distance to classify market regime:

SQUEEZE BB width contracting + price near SMA → Range-bound → Sell short strangles (collect premium)

FIRE BB expanding after squeeze + price trending away from SMA → Breakout → Sell directional option (theta + direction)

Same indicators, opposite signals, complementary trades. Squeeze profits in calm markets, Fire profits in volatile breakouts.
