My ranking

  1. E (hero %) — the one number that actually matters (concentration) gets the visual weight. Cleanest.
  2. C (before → after) — most "interesting" — tells the story of each bucket.
  3. A (two-line block) — most info-dense if you want all facts visible.
  4. B (donut) — novel but donut feels redundant with the bar on the left.
  5. F (trailing chip) — minimal but easy to miss amid the stock chips.
