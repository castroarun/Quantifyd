Why Breakouts Never Triggered

  • 698 consolidation zones found, 0 breakouts confirmed. This explains why R2/R3 topup parameters had zero effect.
  • The breakout criteria are too strict: Requires price > range_high × 1.02 AND volume > 1.5× avg volume, on the same day. These rarely coincide for large-cap Nifty 500 stocks.
  • Momentum stocks move gradually: Portfolio stocks near ATH tend to drift upward rather than gap with volume spikes. The breakout detector is designed for lower-float, high-volatility names.
  • Weekly checking cadence: The engine only checks breakouts every 5 days, so many genuine intraday breakouts are missed by the time the next check occurs.
  • Recommendation: Either (a) relax breakout volume to 1.0x and remove the 2% buffer, or (b) disable topup entirely and use the capital for more initial positions, or (c) redesign as a daily check with lower thresholds.
