Add factor-index rotation study (research/64): factors too correlated to diversify; best book swaps Nifty for the Momentum factor

Follow-on to the GTAA ETF study. Tests whether equal-weighting/rotating the Nifty
factor indices (Momentum, Quality, Value, Low-Vol, Alpha) works like the asset-class
trio. It does not - the factors are 0.65 correlated (mostly the same Nifty bet), so
equal-weight tops out at Calmar 0.76. The better book keeps Gold and Nasdaq for
diversification but upgrades the equity sleeve from Nifty to the Momentum factor,
weighted by inverse-volatility: Calmar 1.77, CAGR 22.1%, drawdown -12.5% - a small but
real improvement on the earlier Nifty book (1.75). Viewable at
/app/backtest/factor-index-rotation.
